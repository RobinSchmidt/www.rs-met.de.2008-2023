On this page you can purchase a license for Straightliner. The price for the license is 89 €.

Money transactions for license purchases are handled through the online payment service 'PayPal' - if you don't know, what PayPal is or how it works, click here to learn more about PayPal. The procedure is as follows: Click the 'Buy Now' button at the bottom of this page and you will be directed to a PayPal payment page where you enter your personal data and initiate the money transfer. Once this transfer has been completed, you will receive a license file via e-mail typically within a day or two (it may take longer when i, for some reason, temporarily don't have access to the internet). This license file must be placed into the same directory where the plugin resides (the one downloadable from here) - the plugin will recognize this license file and switch from demo-mode into unrestricted mode of operation. Note that licenses can not be returned or transferred, so it's highly recommended to thoroughly try out the demo version of the product before you buy a license in order to make sure that the product works for you.
